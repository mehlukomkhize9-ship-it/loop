About N&N Chocolate Factory

Founded in 2021 by Aaliyah Naidoo, N&N Chocolate Factory began as a passion project when Aaliyah's homemade chocolates became a hit at school fairs. Today, we're a family-run business dedicated to creating high-quality, artisanal chocolates using traditional methods with a modern twist.

Our team includes Aaliyah, her husband Tshepisho Ndlovu, and their child Jamie Naidoo Ndlovu, who bring their unique perspectives to our chocolate creations.

Our Digital Transformation

We're implementing cutting-edge digital solutions to streamline our ordering process, improve customer experience, and ensure sustainable business practices. Our new AI-powered ordering system helps us serve you better while maintaining the personal touch of a family business.
